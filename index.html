About Me

I am a Web Developer who is passionate about tech and coding.


With a background in business economics, I have previously worked as an E-Commerce Account Specialist and Inventory Management Specialist at IWV USA for a total of 3 years. I had a fortunate opportunity to be introduced to development and therefore decided to take a big leap for my future career as a Web Developer.


At LearningFuze, I have been trained under a simulated working environment for 800+ hours in the course of 12 weeks. Through this intensive training program, I have not only built a strong foundation in tools and languages but acquired traits essential to becoming a successful Web Developer. With the main focus on JavaScript, HTML, CSS, React.js, and Node.js, I am actively seeking a Junior Developer position. I look forward to further expanding my knowledge in coding and to be working for a great company where I can make a positive impact.
